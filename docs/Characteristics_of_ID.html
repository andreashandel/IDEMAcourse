Characterizing Infection States

Andreas Handel

2020-08-03

Overview

In this module, we will discuss how to characterize the infection states of different ID. We will look at this both from a medical perspective and from a public health perspective and discuss how these perspectives complement each other.

Assignments

Start by reading the “Characterizing Infectious Disease States” chapter of IDEMAB.

The book chapter gives you one view .

For some further discussions, check out the following videos.

The first video is from the “Epidemics” online course. Mary Poss, one of the course instructors, talks about the difference between infection and disease.

The second video is from the HKU Epidemics course sequence. In that video, Joseph Wu from the University of Hong Kong discusses some quantities relating to infection and disease state and duration and their importance for public health. Again, ignore anything we haven’t covered yet.
